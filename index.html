About Me

Domain Hosting & Digital Marketing Expert

I'm Tasnim Ahmed tech entrepreneur and enthusiast from Bangladesh.

Also a student ; who loves to learn something new everyday. I love technology and this is my passion. That's why I always try to gain updated knowledge about technology. And with this love at what time I built my profession, I don't know. Passion and profession both are the same now for me.

Following this, I established my first company HosterPlan,a well-known brand in the domain hosting industry, in 2019 and at the same time I am working as the Chief Executive here. In 2020, I established Marrow IT, a tech solution service provider (Stopped Now). I am also working as Chief Operations Officer at SatisfyHost LTD. After that I joined TOS Technology LTD in early 2021. Here I worked with TOSHost's customer support and digital marketing departments. Since 2022 I have been working as a Business Development Officer for BDIXWebHosting, a sub brand of Blue Dot Technology LTD.
